FAQs

Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time developer?

First off, it costs more than $100,000 a year to hire a full-time senior-level developer, benefits included (and finding one that is available can be difficult). Moreover, there can be times when you don't have a steady workload to keep them busy, which puts you in the awkward position of having to pay for time that isn't used.

On the other hand, our monthly plan gives you freedom by letting you stop and restart your membership whenever you'd like. This guarantees that you pay your designer only when work is actually available, giving you a more flexible and affordable solution.

Is there a limit to how many revisions I can have?

Once subscribed, you're able to add as many revisions as you'd like, and they will be delivered one by one in a timely manner.

How fast will I receive my designs?

It may vary depending on the complexity of your site but on average, most requests are completed in just three days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer.

How much does a Webflow or Framer website cost?

Webflow and Framer has become some of the most popular web design platforms/CMS at the moment, and because of that the demand for Webflow and Framer agencies is very high. Considering public data, hourly rates range from $100 to $200 per hour. This means that a site of 5 to 10 pages may cost around $15 to $30k.

How long does it take to build a Webflow or Framer website?

Project durations usually depend a lot on the scope, however, Webflow and Framer is very popular because of the agile form of development, so it takes less time than other platforms like WordPress. The average timing for these projects could range from 1 week to 8 weeks. However, the final range will depend on your specific website scope.

What is the cost to maintain a Webflow or Framer website?

Unlike many other CMS, Webflow and Framer do not require so many updates and security patches, because all of these are managed automatically by the Webflow and Framer platforms the maintenance costs required are very low.

The only costs associated to keeping the website running are the Webflow or Framer hosting and account membership which range from $5 to $100 per month.

Are there any refunds if I don't like the service?

Unfortunately, due to the nature of the service there are no refunds.
